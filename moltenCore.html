MOLTEN CORE

Non decaying rolling system: you always roll 100.

Rules

- There is NOT a "max epic limit/loot limit" per person.
- You will have time until raid invites to reserve items.
- Once the invites are out reservations will be locked and no new reservations will be accepted.
- A player can choose to reserve 2 items, no items or twice the same item.

What happens when an item drops?

- If the item IS NOT reserved by anyone, everyone can roll for their main spec.
- If the item IS on the reserve list of ONE player, that player will get it by default.
- If the item IS on the reserve list of two or more players, those players will be eligible to roll on the item.
- If the item has been reserved TWICE by one player, that player is eligible to roll the item twice and only the best roll will be taken in account.
- If two of the same item will drop and multiple players have reserved it, item will be assigned to the top two rolls.

Mats & BOEs:

- Only BOE set items can be reserved.
- Mats and boe will go to the guild bank and distributed for free as needed.
- Disenchanted for mats (and free enchanting mats given for free) or sold on the ah if the bank is overstocked.
- Blue BOEs can be requested for free from players if they are an upgrade for their gear.

Legendaries:

- Bindings of the Windseeker -> Antiklesys first set
- Eye of sulfuras -> Officers discussing on it.
- Atiesh -> TBD in P5/P6
